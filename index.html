The Shape

      CONFUSED! So dreadfully confused I am now that I cannot even trust myself. I was found delirious near his corpse, but does this mean that it was me who murdered him, struck his head ruthlessly with an axe? Yes, I loathed him so much and with all my heart. I resented his success and cursed his future. Every circumstantial evidence ensures that the brutal murderer was me, but I remember something different --clearly different about the day, and the whole tragedy I underwent with extreme torture.
It was the summer of three years ago that my life diverged from its way toward hopeless destruction. After the last letter arrived for me, my father disappeared abruptly without a trace. In the letter, it was written that he ruined his business and now is unable to support me. It took not so much time until I realized what this really meant as I soon went out of money and had to pawn my possessions to feed myself.
